Three ways to keep a stub.

The static design explorations behind the app. A one-page shelf for the ticket stubs you held on to, filed by kind, with an Upcoming tab. Tap a stub to light it up and pull out the original ticket. Same memories in each, three feelings.

AThe Vintage Shelf, lightWarm paper, torn stubs on a tilt, a soft amber glow. The drawer you kept them in.open -> BThe Vintage Shelf, darkThe same shelf after dark. Same stubs, same glow, on warm near-black.open -> CThe Editorial WallOversized type, a filed grid, electric accent. The stubs as a printed index.open ->
